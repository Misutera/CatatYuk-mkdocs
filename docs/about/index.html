About Us

Our Mission

At CatatYuk, we're on a mission to democratize financial management for small businesses. We believe every entrepreneur should have access to simple yet powerful financial tools to make confident decisions — regardless of accounting background.

◎

Our Vision

To become the go-to financial management solution for MSMEs across the region — helping small business owners make informed financial decisions with confidence.

♥

Our Values

Simplicity in design and function, accuracy in financial reporting, accessibility for all business owners, continuous improvement and innovation.
