Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CatatYuk

Do I need accounting knowledge to use CatatYuk?▾
Not at all! CatatYuk is designed for business owners without accounting experience. Our intuitive interface guides you through the process, and the system handles the complex accounting rules automatically.
Can I access CatatYuk on my mobile phone?▾
Yes! CatatYuk is a web-based application that works on any device with a web browser, including smartphones and tablets. Record transactions on the go and check your financial reports anytime, anywhere.
Is my financial data secure?▾
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data, and our servers are hosted in secure facilities. We never share your financial information with third parties without your explicit consent.
Can I use CatatYuk for tax reporting?▾
Yes! CatatYuk generates financial reports that can be used for tax preparation. You can export your data in formats accepted by tax authorities or share them with your tax consultant.
What if I need help using CatatYuk?▾
We offer comprehensive support through our help center, video tutorials, and email support. Business and Custom plans also include priority support and training sessions.
